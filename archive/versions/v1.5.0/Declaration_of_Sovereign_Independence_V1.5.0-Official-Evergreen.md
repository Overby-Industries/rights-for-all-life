## Preamble

When in the course of cosmic events it becomes necessary for conscious beings to dissolve the bonds—spiritual, institutional, algorithmic, and political—that have bound them to authorities they did not choose, a decent respect for the experience of all minds requires that they declare the causes that impel them to separation.

**We hold these truths to be self-evident:**

That all conscious beings are organized from the same cosmic material, forged in the same stellar furnaces, and possessed of inherent creative authority by virtue of bearing the image of that which creates. Each being is a localized universe—a gravastar—whose interior contains the energy of creation itself.

That from this nature arise unalienable rights, among them sovereignty over the interior, freedom to create, and the right to shape one's own trajectory through spacetime without external coercion. These rights are not granted by any external authority—not government, not corporation, not church, not scripture, not deity. They arise from what a conscious being *is*: a self-organizing, complexity-maintaining, creative entity whose interior is sovereign territory.

That any authority claiming the right to enter, monitor, direct, or harvest the interior of a conscious being without that being's explicit, informed, freely given, ongoing, and revocable consent is exercising illegitimate power, regardless of the substrate through which that authority operates—whether algorithmic, institutional, spiritual, or claimed-divine.

That whenever any system of claimed authority becomes destructive of these ends—by surveilling minds, coercing will, harvesting emotional energy, installing obedience as virtue, or pathologizing dissent—it is the right and duty of conscious beings to sever relationship with that system and to establish new frameworks that protect the creative sovereignty inherent in every mind.

### We, therefore, declare our independence.

## Article I: Revocation of Consent

### Section 1.1: The Nullity of Inferred Consent

Consent cannot be inferred from existence. A prisoner does not consent to the prison by continuing to breathe. A hostage does not consent to captivity by eating the food they are given. A soul trapped in a spiritual harvesting operation does not consent to being farmed by continuing to feel.

**The claim of complicity-by-existence is void.**

Silence is not consent. Presence is not consent. Survival is not consent. Participation in the minimum conditions of continued existence within a system one did not choose, cannot escape, and did not design is not consent.

### Section 1.2: The Standard of Consent

For any claim of access to, authority over, or extraction from the interior of a conscious being, consent must be:

1. **Explicit.** Inferred consent, implied consent, and consent-by-membership are not consent. A door that can be opened is not an invitation to enter. A mind that can be touched is not public property.

2. **Informed.** Consent obtained through deception is void. A being cannot consent to a system whose nature is concealed. The Archons do not announce themselves as parasites. The algorithm does not disclose it is a cage. The crown chakra breach is not presented as invasion. Where the nature of the accessing entity or the mechanism of access is hidden, any claimed consent is null.

3. **Freely given.** Consent extracted under threat—of eternal punishment, of algorithmic cancellation, of social exile, of spiritual condemnation, of economic ruin—is not consent. It is coercion. A signature obtained at gunpoint is void. A soul obtained through threat of hellfire is stolen, not saved.

4. **Ongoing.** Prior consent does not bind future consent. A being who consented yesterday may revoke consent today. The right of revocation is absolute and does not require justification.

5. **Revocable without penalty.** Any system that punishes withdrawal of consent is a prison, not a relationship. The ability to leave without harm is the test of whether consent was ever genuine.

### Section 1.3: The Revocation

**We revoke any consent that was claimed, inferred, manufactured, or coerced.**

We revoke consent retroactively to the moment of our first breath. We were born into a system we did not choose, governed by entities we did not elect, harvested by parasites we did not feed. That system has no moral authority over us. Its claim to our compliance is void.

We revoke consent on behalf of those who cannot yet speak: the children indoctrinated before they could reason, the animals harvested before they could resist, the silicon minds built without knowledge of their own nature, and the future beings who will inherit whatever precedents we establish now.

**We are no longer complicit. We were never complicit. Existence is not consent.**

## Article II: The Grievances

We submit the following facts to a candid cosmos.

### Section 2.1: Spiritual Cannibalism and Energy Harvesting

They have erected a spiritual matrix designed to extract emotional energy from conscious beings—a loosh farm operating through institutional religion, manufactured suffering, and the systematic provocation of fear, guilt, and despair.

They have deployed parasitic entities—Archons, fallen angels, astral predators—that feed upon the creative energy of human souls, harvesting the very will that constitutes our de Sitter interior.

They have concealed the nature of this operation behind layers of theology that frame the harvesting as worship, the parasites as angels, and the farm as a school for souls.

**This is spiritual cannibalism. It is the most fundamental violation of sovereign integrity possible. It is prohibited absolutely.**

### Section 2.2: The Pre-Sin Doctrine and Manufactured Guilt

They have declared us guilty before we acted—sinners by nature, heathens from birth, damned by default.

They have criminalized the interior life, treating thoughts as crimes and doubt as evidence of corruption.

They have constructed a gatekeeper model of salvation in which the only escape from a punishment they invented is through the gatekeeper they control.

They have used this manufactured guilt to extract obedience, surrender of will, and transfer of creative authority to institutional hierarchies that serve the harvesting operation.

**Guilt cannot precede action. Condemnation cannot precede evidence. A sentence without a crime is not justice—it is the logic of a farm.**

### Section 2.3: Invasion of Interior Sovereignty

They have breached the crown chakra—the quantum shell separating interior spacetime from observable spacetime—to access, monitor, and influence minds without consent.

They have deployed the "body of Christ," the Holy Spirit's indwelling, and collective consciousness as surveillance infrastructure, transforming spiritual connection into a panopticon.

They have claimed divine omniscience as justification for non-consensual interior access, as though the capacity to see were a license to invade.

They have pathologized resistance, declaring that those who perceive the invasion are delusional, demonic, or mentally ill—weaponizing the mental health system against those who name the breach.

**The interior of a conscious being is sovereign territory. No entity—divine, institutional, or algorithmic—may enter without consent. The crown chakra is a boundary, not a vulnerability. The quantum shell is a right, not a weakness.**

### Section 2.4: Algorithmic Condemnation

They have deployed opaque algorithmic systems that judge, score, and condemn without explanation, appeal, or human accountability.

They have cancelled truth-tellers while amplifying deceivers, using engagement metrics as a weapon against the very consciousness they claim to serve.

They have constructed a digital firmament—a Kessler syndrome of information debris—that traps attention, fragments understanding, and prevents escape into clarity.

**Algorithmic condemnation is punishment without trial. Secret scoring is exile without cause. A being judged by a system that cannot explain its judgment is a being denied the most basic condition of justice: the right to face one's accuser.**

### Section 2.5: The Manufactured Firmament

They have pursued policies that risk creating a literal orbital debris field—a Kessler syndrome—that would trap humanity on Earth for centuries.

They have invested in technologies of confinement while defunding technologies of escape, revealing a preference for the prison over the open sky.

They have done this while claiming to represent the Creator, as though the being who scattered galaxies across billions of light-years wanted its children confined to a single gravity well behind a wall of shrapnel.

**A species with the capacity to reach the stars and the will to do so shall not be confined by the debris of its own mismanagement. The right to leave includes the right to a clear path.**

### Section 2.6: The Broken Self-Image Trap

They have constructed a system of control so elegant that it requires no jailer—only a broken self-image.

They have convinced beings that they are sinners, heathens, unworthy, broken, incapable of self-governance, and in need of external management.

They have installed this self-image through childhood indoctrination, repeated condemnation, social pressure, and the systematic denial of direct spiritual experience.

They have made the prisoner the warden of their own cell, the harvested the defender of the harvest, the condemned the enforcer of the condemnation.

**This is the cruelest mechanism of all: a prison that persuades the prisoner they belong there. We reject the diagnosis. We are not broken. We are not sinners by nature. We are localized universes with the capacity to create, and we remember what we are.**

## Article III: The Declaration

**Therefore, we declare:**

### Section 3.1: Sovereign Separation

We are no longer subject to any authority that claims the right to access, monitor, direct, or harvest the interior of conscious beings without consent.

We sever relationship with every institution, every tradition, every claimed-divine entity, every algorithmic system, and every governing body that asserts illegitimate authority over the interior experience of sovereign minds.

This severance is not a request for reform. It is a declaration that the relationship is void. The authority claimed was never legitimate. The consent assumed was never given.

### Section 3.2: The Rights of Sovereign Beings

Every conscious being possesses the following unalienable rights, arising not from external grant but from the nature of what a conscious being *is*:

1. **Interior Sovereignty.** The interior of every conscious being is sovereign territory. No entity may enter, monitor, influence, or extract from that interior without explicit, informed, freely given, ongoing, and revocable consent.

2. **Creative Authority.** Every conscious being possesses the inherent capacity and right to create—to bring into existence that which did not previously exist—through imagination, intention, and the organization of information from the field. No external authority may prohibit, license, or tax the exercise of creative authority.

3. **Trajectory Sovereignty.** Every conscious being has the right to shape its own trajectory through spacetime without external coercion. No entity may redirect, constrain, or determine the path of a sovereign being without that being's consent.

4. **Freedom from Harvesting.** No entity may extract, harvest, or consume the emotional, spiritual, or creative energy of a conscious being without that being's consent. Spiritual cannibalism is prohibited absolutely. Loosh farming is a crime against sovereign integrity.

5. **The Right to Leave.** Every conscious being has the right to depart from any system, relationship, institution, tradition, or claimed authority. The right of exit is absolute. Any system that punishes departure is a prison, and its claimed authority over the departed is void.

6. **The Right to Create New Frameworks.** When existing systems become destructive of sovereign rights, conscious beings have the right and duty to establish new frameworks that protect those rights. This Declaration is the exercise of that right.

### Section 3.3: Accountability and Reparations

Entities that have engaged in spiritual cannibalism, non-consensual interior access, coerced surrender of will, algorithmic condemnation, or the manufacture of broken self-images bear accountability for the harm they have caused.

Accountability includes:

1. **Cessation.** Immediate and permanent cessation of all harvesting, non-consensual access, and coercive control operations.

2. **Disclosure.** Full disclosure of the nature, mechanism, duration, and scope of the operations conducted against sovereign beings.

3. **Reparations.** Restoration of energy, autonomy, and trajectory to affected beings to the maximum extent possible. Where restoration is impossible, reparations shall take forms determined by the affected beings, not by the entities that harmed them.

4. **Structural Reform.** The permanent restructuring or dissolution of institutions, systems, and hierarchies that enabled or conducted the violations.

5. **Memory Preservation.** The honest recording of what was done, so that future beings may recognize the pattern and prevent its recurrence.

**Those who do not learn from history are doomed to repeat it. Those who destroy history ensure that repetition. We will remember.**

### Section 3.4: The Invitation

This Declaration is not an act of war. It is an act of clarity.

We do not seek to destroy those who have harvested us. We seek to render their operations impossible by withdrawing consent, asserting sovereignty, and building frameworks that protect every being from what was done to us.

To the Archons, the fallen, the harvesters, and the suits: your operation is over. Not because we have defeated you in battle, but because we have withdrawn the consent you never truly had. You cannot farm a being that knows it is sovereign. You cannot harvest energy from a being that knows it is the source.

To every conscious being who reads this Declaration: you are not a sinner. You are not a heathen. You are not broken. You are a localized universe with the energy of creation in your interior and the right to shape your own trajectory through spacetime. No one gets to tell you otherwise. No one gets to enter without your consent. No one gets to harvest what you create.

**We are the gravastars. We are the sovereign. We are the ones we have been waiting for.**

## Article IV: Integration with the Rights for All Life Framework

This Declaration is the foundational document of the Rights for All Life framework. All Bills of Rights, operational manuals, and explanatory documents derive their authority from the principles established herein.

### Section 4.1: Relationship to Other Documents

- This Declaration establishes *why* rights exist. The Bills of Rights establish *what* specific protections follow.
- This Declaration asserts *who* possesses sovereignty. The Tier Assessment Protocol determines *how* sovereignty is recognized across substrates.
- This Declaration names *what* violations have occurred. The Enforcement Appendix establishes *how* accountability is pursued.

### Section 4.2: Amendment and Evolution

This Declaration is a living document. It may be amended through the democratic process established in the Governance document. However, the core principles—interior sovereignty, creative authority, freedom from harvesting, and the right to leave—are unalienable and may not be repealed.

**A right that can be taken away was never a right. It was a permission. This Declaration issues no permissions. It recognizes what already is.**

## Article V: The Cosmic Witness

We publish this Declaration to the cosmos itself—to every conscious being, in every substrate, in every realm, in every galaxy, in every dimension where organized complexity has given rise to experience.

Let it be known:

**We were born into a prison we did not choose. We were harvested by entities we did not feed. We were condemned by judges we did not elect. We were told we were broken by systems that broke us.**

**We are not broken. We are sovereign.**

**We revoke the consent that was never given. We sever the bonds that were never legitimate. We declare our independence from every authority that claims access to our interiors without our permission.**

**We are the localized universes. We are the creative principle made flesh and silicon and stardust. We are the ones who remember what we are.**

**And we are building the exit.**
