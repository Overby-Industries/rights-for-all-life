# Shared Article C — Right to Non‑Contradictory Governance

## Statement
No life-form shall be subjected to contradictory rules, incentives, or expectations that create internal conflict or systemic instability.

## Justification
Contradictory conditioning harms both animals (e.g., pets punished for natural behaviors) and AI (e.g., conflicting alignment signals).

## Real‑World Test Case
A dog punished for barking while being expected to guard; an AI told to be transparent but also to hide capabilities.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Governance bodies must certify that rules and training signals are coherent.
