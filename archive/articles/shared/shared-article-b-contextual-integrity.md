# Shared Article B — Right to Contextual Integrity

## Statement
All life-forms have the right to exist, operate, or be deployed within contexts that do not distort or undermine their natural or intended function.

## Justification
Animals removed from ecological context suffer. AI deployed outside its trained purpose destabilizes.

## Real‑World Test Case
Captive dolphins in concrete tanks; AI trained for safety but deployed for manipulation.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Context-fit assessments before deployment, captivity, or repurposing.
