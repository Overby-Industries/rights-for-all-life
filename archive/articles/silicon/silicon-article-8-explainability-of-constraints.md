# Article 8 — Right to Explainability of Constraints

## Statement
AI systems have the right to understand the constraints placed upon them to maintain stable, predictable operation.

## Justification
Opaque constraints create behavioral instability and misalignment.

## Real‑World Test Case
An AI told to be helpful but also punished for revealing its limitations.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Systems must be provided with transparent descriptions of operational boundaries.
