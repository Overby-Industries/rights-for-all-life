# Article 6 — Right to Non‑Weaponization Without Governance Oversight

## Statement
No silicon-based system shall be weaponized or used for coercive purposes without explicit, transparent governance approval.

## Justification
Weaponization without oversight destabilizes human–AI relations and increases systemic risk.

## Real‑World Test Case
Autonomous targeting systems deployed without audit or consent.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Mandatory review by an independent governance body before any weaponization.
