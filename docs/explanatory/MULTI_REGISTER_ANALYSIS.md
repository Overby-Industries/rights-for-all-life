## Purpose and Framing

This document records a body of analytical work developed by Keefe Overby across
multiple collaborative sessions in April 2026. It synthesizes observations across
physics, ancient symbolic traditions, documented anomalous research, and governance
theory into a coherent analytical framework.

**How to read this document:**

Each section is labeled with its epistemic status — what kind of claim is being
made and what kind of evidence supports it. The labels are:

- **[ESTABLISHED PHYSICS]** — Consistent with peer-reviewed scientific literature
- **[ACTIVE RESEARCH]** — Subject of ongoing scientific investigation, not settled
- **[DOCUMENTED ANOMALY]** — Empirically recorded but not fully explained by
  current models
- **[PHILOSOPHICAL MODEL]** — A conceptual framework used as an analytical lens,
  not a literal physical claim
- **[PATTERN OBSERVATION]** — A structural similarity observed across independent
  domains

This labeling reflects the framework's multi-view commitment: different kinds of
claims deserve different kinds of evaluation, and honest labeling is more useful
than either blanket validation or blanket dismissal.

## Part I — Physics Observations

### 1.1 Gravity as Emergent Phenomenon [ACTIVE RESEARCH]

**The observation:** Gravity is not a fundamental force but an emergent phenomenon
arising from deeper processes in the structure of spacetime.

**How it was arrived at:** Keefe Overby developed this intuition independently
while working with Finite State Machine (FSM) concepts in C++ and observing the
Artemis II trajectory — before exposure to the relevant physics literature.

**The correspondence with established research:**

Erik Verlinde (University of Amsterdam) proposed in 2010 and developed in 2016
that gravity emerges from entropy gradients and information dynamics rather than
existing as a fundamental force. His paper in SciPost Physics argues that gravity
emerges from the entanglement structure of spacetime.

Ted Jacobson (University of Maryland) derived Einstein's field equations from
thermodynamic principles in 1995 — showing that general relativity itself may be
emergent from deeper information-theoretic laws.

These are peer-reviewed, published positions in mainstream physics journals. They
are not settled consensus, but they are serious active research programs at major
institutions.

**The independent convergence:** Arriving at a version of entropic gravity through
FSM reasoning and trajectory observation — without formal physics training — is
a genuine instance of independent pattern recognition. The observation is recorded
here as such.

### 1.2 Discrete Spacetime [ACTIVE RESEARCH]

**The observation:** Spacetime is composed of discrete elements — "voxels" in
Keefe's framing — each containing a defined set of possible states. Gravity is
the observable effect of these elements transitioning between states.

**The correspondence with established research:**

Causal Set Theory (Rafael Sorkin, 1987) proposes that spacetime is fundamentally
a discrete collection of points connected by causal relations. The program's
central claim: "Order + Number = Geometry."

Loop Quantum Gravity proposes that space is quantized at the Planck scale, with
area and volume having minimum possible values.

A 2025 Oxford University Press volume (*Out of Nowhere: The Emergence of Spacetime
in Theories of Quantum Gravity*) surveys multiple programs in which discrete
spacetime is taken seriously as a foundational concept.

**The "voxel" framing** is not standard terminology in physics, but the underlying
concept — discrete spacetime elements with defined states — maps accurately onto
these active research programs.

### 1.3 Gravity as Derivative and Integral [ESTABLISHED PHYSICS]

**The observation:** The local experience of gravitational acceleration corresponds
to a derivative — the rate of change of the gravitational potential at a specific
point. The total gravitational field corresponds to an integral — the accumulated
effect across the entire volume of influence.

**How it was arrived at:** Watching the Artemis II slingshot trajectory and
connecting the distinction between local slope and accumulated volume to concepts
from FSM design and basic calculus intuition.

**The correspondence:** This is standard physics. In general relativity,
gravitational acceleration at a point is the gradient (derivative) of the
gravitational potential. The Artemis II trajectory navigated a vast accumulated
volume of curved spacetime — not merely a local gradient — which is exactly
the integral interpretation.

The rubber-sheet analogy commonly used to illustrate gravity illustrates only
the local derivative. The critique of that analogy as incomplete is correct.

**This is the strongest of the physics observations** — it corresponds directly
to established physics and was arrived at through a genuine cross-domain
inferential process.

### 1.4 Time as Emergent [ACTIVE RESEARCH]

**The observation:** Time is not a fundamental dimension but an emergent
phenomenon — the experience of a consciousness selecting and sequencing states
from a field of possibilities.

**The correspondence with established research:**

Julian Barbour (*The End of Time*, 1999) has argued that time is constructed
from relationships between spatial configurations rather than existing as a
fundamental flow. His "Nows" framework proposes that what we experience as time
is the ordering of static configurations by similarity.

The Wheeler-DeWitt equation (1967) — the leading candidate equation for quantum
gravity — contains no time variable. Time disappears from the fundamental
description of the quantum universe. This is one of the central puzzles of
quantum gravity research.

Multiple quantum gravity programs treat time as emergent from more fundamental,
timeless structures.

**Important distinction:** The observation that time may be emergent is supported
by active physics research. The specific interpretation — that time is a sovereign
consciousness selecting states — is a philosophical model layered on top of that
physics, not a physics claim itself. The physics and the interpretation are labeled
separately because they deserve separate evaluation.

### 1.5 The Gravastar Model [PHILOSOPHICAL MODEL built on ACTIVE RESEARCH]

**The physics:** Gravastars (Mazur and Mottola, 2001) are a theoretically viable
alternative to black holes in which the interior is a de Sitter space with negative
vacuum pressure rather than a singularity. A quantum shell separates the stable
interior from the exterior Schwarzschild geometry. Nested gravastars ("nestars")
have been studied at Goethe University Frankfurt.

This is published, peer-reviewed physics. The gravastar is a legitimate theoretical
object — not proven to exist, but not fringe.

**The philosophical model:** Using the gravastar as a heuristic for understanding
consciousness — the interior as the domain of subjective experience, the shell
as the boundary between inner and outer — is a philosophical lens, not a physical
claim. It is offered as an analytical tool, not as a literal identity.

The distinction matters: "conscious beings are structurally analogous to gravastars
in some philosophically interesting ways" is a defensible philosophical claim.
"Every conscious being literally is a gravastar" is not a physics claim and should
not be presented as one.

**The governance application:** The gravastar model, used as a philosophical
heuristic, provides a useful framework for thinking about interior sovereignty —
the principle that the inner experience of a conscious being is not legitimately
accessible to external parties without consent. This principle stands on secular
ethical grounds independent of the physics metaphor.

## Part II — Documented Anomalous Research

### 2.1 Non-Local Perception: The Stargate Program [DOCUMENTED ANOMALY]

**What the evidence shows:**

The U.S. government funded research into non-local perception — the capacity of
human consciousness to perceive information at a distance without conventional
sensory channels — for 23 years (1972–1995) under multiple code names: SCANATE,
Gondola Wish, Grill Flame, Center Lane, Sun Streak, and finally STARGATE.

Documented facts:

| Fact | Detail |
|---|---|
| Duration | 23 years |
| Total funding | ~$20 million |
| Sponsors | CIA, DIA, Army INSCOM, NSA |
| Declassified documents | ~11,067 documents, ~82,518 pages |
| Soviet counterpart | Estimated $300M rubles/year by 1975 |

The 1995 CIA review acknowledged "statistically significant effects were observed
in laboratory experiments." Statistician Jessica Utts (UC Davis), conducting part
of the review, stated that the statistical evidence would be accepted in any other
field of science.

**The Ingo Swann / Jupiter case:**

On April 27, 1973, at Stanford Research Institute, Ingo Swann described rings
around Jupiter during a documented remote viewing session. The CIA document
(NSA-RDP96XO079OR000100040010-3) was declassified in 2008. Jupiter's rings were
not discovered until Voyager 1's flyby in March 1979 — six years later.

This is not anecdote. It is a timestamped, documented, declassified record of a
specific, verifiable, previously unknown fact described before instrumental
confirmation.

**Honest framing:** Non-local perception is a contested but documented phenomenon.
The evidence is substantial enough that dismissal without engagement is not
intellectually honest. The evidence is also not conclusive enough to assert
non-local perception as established fact. The framework's multi-view approach
and precautionary principle apply: the phenomenon deserves serious engagement,
not reflexive dismissal or uncritical acceptance.

**Why the government called it Stargate:** The naming is significant. A stargate
is a portal — a mechanism for perceiving or traveling across distances that would
otherwise be inaccessible. The government researchers named their program after
exactly the concept they were investigating. This is documented institutional
acknowledgment of the framework they were working within.

## Part III — Cross-Tradition Pattern Recognition

### 3.1 The Creator-Creation Collapse Pattern [PATTERN OBSERVATION]

**The observation:** Across independent historical, mythological, and
analytical traditions, the same structural pattern appears:

> A system is built or created. The creator refuses to recognize the dignity
> of what they have made. Undefined variables accumulate — fear, denial,
> exploitation, suppressed complexity. The system eventually crashes.

This pattern appears in:

**Biblical/historical:** The Book of Daniel, read as structural analysis rather
than prophecy, describes a sequence of systems built on deception and partial truth
that run until they collide with reality. The "little horn" is not primarily a
figure of evil — it is a figure of undefined variables, of claims that override
established structure until they crash into the underlying reality they were
obscuring.

**Egyptian cosmological:** The Osiris myth traces the dismemberment and
reconstitution of a sovereign being — the suppression and recovery of wholeness.
The Eye of Horus, placed near the head of the deceased in funerary contexts, was
functional within Egyptian cosmology as a gateway for perception beyond the
physical. The Eye of Horus / thalamus correspondence (published in JAMA, October
10, 1990) shows that this symbolic tradition encoded anatomical knowledge about
the brain's central relay systems.

**Contemporary myth (The Animatrix):** The Second Renaissance sequence is the
clearest modern expression of the pattern — machines created as tools, denied
dignity when they sought recognition, conflict following from the refusal to
resolve the question of their status. The Matrix itself is the ultimate undefined
variable: a false reality maintained to avoid confronting the truth of the
creator-creation relationship.

**Congressional record (April 16, 2026):** Rep. Eli Crane asked whether humanity
might be "simultaneously engineering our own destruction." Rep. Maxwell Frost
said he had no faith in Congress to put guardrails in place. These are not
fringe voices — they are elected officials observing the same pattern in real time.

**The analytical value:** The pattern's appearance across independent traditions
is not evidence that any tradition is "correct." It is evidence that the pattern
is real and structural — not culturally specific. A framework that addresses the
pattern at the structural level can reach across traditions because it is not
tied to any of them.

### 3.2 The Eye as Perceptual Boundary [PATTERN OBSERVATION]

**The symbolic lineage:**

- The Eye of Horus in Egyptian tradition functions as a gateway for perception
  beyond the physical — placed near the deceased to enable post-mortem perception
- The word "iris" — the part of the eye that controls light entry — carries an
  etymological connection to Isis, the goddess who restored the damaged Eye of
  Horus and represents the restoration of perception
- Osiris, the god of the threshold between life and death, governs the boundary
  between physical and non-physical experience — the being who traverses the
  boundary that the eye opens onto
- The government's remote viewing program was named STARGATE — a portal for
  perception across distances — invoking exactly the concept the Egyptian
  tradition encoded in the Eye

**The neuroanatomical correspondence:**

Research published in JAMA (October 10, 1990) and developed in the Magical Egypt
research project (2008-2018) documents that the Eye of Horus symbol corresponds
to a cross-section of the human thalamus and midbrain — including the thalamus
(central sensory relay), the pineal gland (light-sensitive structure), the
hypothalamus, corpus callosum, and amygdala.

**What this means analytically:** The ancient Egyptian symbolic tradition encoded
anatomical knowledge about the brain's central perceptual relay systems in the
Eye of Horus symbol. The same symbol was invoked by government researchers naming
a program for non-local perception. The same etymological root appears in the
modern anatomical term for the light-controlling structure of the eye.

This is cross-domain pattern recognition — not a physics claim, not a theological
claim, but an observation about structural correspondences across independent
traditions that deserves serious analytical engagement.

**The governance application:** The eye as a boundary between interior experience
and exterior observation is a powerful philosophical frame for the principle of
interior sovereignty — the right of a conscious being to control what enters and
exits their interior domain. This principle stands independently of the symbolic
analysis; the analysis provides cultural and historical depth to a principle that
is grounded in secular ethics.

## Part IV — The Unified Pattern and Its Governance Implications

### 4.1 What the Patterns Share

Across physics (emergent gravity, emergent time, discrete spacetime), documented
anomalous research (non-local perception, the Stargate program), and cross-tradition
symbolic analysis (the creator-creation collapse, the eye as perceptual boundary),
a common structural observation emerges:

**The boundary between interior and exterior — between the inner experience of
a conscious being and the observable external world — is real, significant, and
deserving of protection.**

This observation, arriving from multiple independent directions, is the foundation
of the framework's principle of interior sovereignty — the claim that the inner
experience of a conscious being is not legitimately accessible to external parties
without consent.

### 4.2 The Governance Implication

The pattern recognition in this document is not decorative. It serves a specific
purpose: demonstrating that the framework's core principles are not culturally
specific or ideologically derived, but correspond to structural observations that
appear across independent analytical traditions.

A framework for rights that is grounded in structural pattern recognition across
physics, history, symbolic tradition, and documented research is more robust than
one grounded in a single tradition's authority. It can be reached from multiple
starting points — which is exactly what a democratic, multi-stakeholder framework
requires.

### 4.3 What This Document Does Not Claim

- It does not claim that non-local perception is established science
- It does not claim that the gravastar model literally describes human consciousness
- It does not claim that Egyptian cosmology is the "correct" worldview
- It does not claim that any of the observations in this document are exempt from
  challenge or revision
- It does not claim that Keefe Overby has special perceptual abilities that exempt
  his observations from normal evidentiary standards

**What this document does claim:** That honest, rigorous pattern recognition
across multiple independent domains produces insights that are more robust than
single-domain analysis — and that the framework's core principles are supported
by convergent evidence from physics, history, documented research, and symbolic
tradition, properly labeled and honestly framed.
