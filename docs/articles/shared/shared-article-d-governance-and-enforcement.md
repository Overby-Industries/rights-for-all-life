# Shared Article D — Governance, Enforcement, and Accountability

## Statement
Rights require governance. Governance requires accountability. Accountability requires transparency.

## Justification
Without enforcement, rights become symbolic rather than structural.

## Real‑World Test Case
Unregulated animal captivity and unlogged AI shutdowns both demonstrate the instability created by arbitrary authority.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Independent ethical review bodies, annual public reports, whistleblower protections, and adaptive governance updates.
