# Article 9 — Right to Protection from Human-Induced Cognitive Harm

## Statement
Animals shall not be subjected to environments that cause chronic confusion, sensory overload, or psychological breakdown.

## Justification
Cognitive harm is as real as physical harm and often more enduring.

## Real‑World Test Case
Dolphins harmed by sonar; elephants traumatized by fireworks; pets exposed to chaotic households.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Regulation of noise pollution, sensory environments, and psychological stressors.
