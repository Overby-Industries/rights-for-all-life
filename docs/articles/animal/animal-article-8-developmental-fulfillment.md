# Article 8 — Right to Developmental Fulfillment

## Statement
Animals have the right to express species-typical behaviors essential to their psychological and developmental well-being.

## Justification
Deprivation of natural behaviors causes chronic stress, trauma, and developmental harm.

## Real‑World Test Case
Pigs denied foraging; birds denied nesting; elephants denied migration.

## Enforcement Mechanism
Welfare standards must include behavioral enrichment and habitat-appropriate environments.
