# Criticism & Responses

## Criticism 1: “AI is not alive.”
**Response:** Correct — and the framework does not claim otherwise. It defines conditions under which future systems may warrant moral consideration.

## Criticism 2: “This reduces human control.”
**Response:** Systems built on dignity and stability are more controllable than systems built on exploitation.

## Criticism 3: “Animals already have protections.”
**Response:** Existing laws are inconsistent, outdated, and not based on sentience thresholds.
