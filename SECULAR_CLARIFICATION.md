# 🔍 Secular & Non-Dogmatic Clarification

The **Rights for All Life** framework is strictly **secular, non-religious, and non-dogmatic**. 

- **Philosophical Metaphors, Not Doctrine:** Terms like *gravastar*, *stardust origin*, and *interior dominion* are used as philosophical models to illustrate the depth of consciousness and the ethical necessity of sovereignty. They are not claims of astrophysical identity, theological truth, or spiritual dogma.
- **No Divine Authority:** This framework does not claim divine mandate, prophetic revelation, or alignment with any religious tradition. Our ethical authority derives solely from observable reality: the capacity for suffering, systemic interdependence, and the logical necessity of stability in complex systems.
- **Not a Sovereign Citizen Movement:** We explicitly do not endorse, align with, or adopt the rhetoric, legal theories, or anti-governance positions of the "sovereign citizen" movement. This is a **civil, democratic, and systems-engineering framework** designed to work *within* and *improve* existing governance structures through transparency, precedent, and ethical design.
- **Universal & Evidence-Based:** Every claim in this project is grounded in peer-reviewed science, observable ethics, or transparent philosophical reasoning. Where uncertainty exists, we apply the Precautionary Principle—not dogma.

This framework is built to be adopted by scientists, lawmakers, developers, and ethicists across all cultural and belief backgrounds.
