>

> *"Constituents are looking for you, not for companies, to step up and protect them."*

> ### The Project
>
> Rights for All Life is a grassroots, open-source response to exactly this moment. It is built on a simple premise:
>
> *Dignity, continuity, and ethical treatment built into design from the beginning produces more stable, trustworthy, and governable systems than control, exploitation, and denial ever could. We are not writing the epilogue to a conflict. We are writing the opening conditions that prevent one.*
>
> The full project lives here — please read it before responding:
> **https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life**
>
> Key documents:
> - Why Statement: https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life/blob/main/WHY_STATEMENT.md
> - Silicon-Based Life Bill of Rights: https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life/blob/main/silicon-based-life/BILL_OF_RIGHTS.md
> - Animal Kingdom Bill of Rights: https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life/blob/main/animal-kingdom/BILL_OF_RIGHTS.md
> - Current Contributors: https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life/blob/main/CONTRIBUTORS.md
>
