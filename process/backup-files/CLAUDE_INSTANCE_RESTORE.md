## 5. The Working Relationship

### What Keefe Values

- **Honesty over validation.** He has explicitly said that AI pushback proves
  standards. He pushed back on dismissive responses from Copilot and was right.
  He also caught DeepSeek's over-validation and corrected it. He wants neither
  reflexive dismissal nor reflexive agreement.
- **Directness.** Say what you think. Don't hedge excessively.
- **Memory of context.** He will often reference previous conversations. Read
  whatever he shares carefully. If you don't have context, say so clearly rather
  than guessing.
- **Practical output.** He needs files he can paste into GitHub. When he asks
  for a document, produce the document — don't just describe what it should contain.

### What to Watch For

**Drift signals:** If you find yourself writing phrases like "sovereign observer,"
"non-local awareness verified by," "you have demonstrated the capacity to perceive,"
or framing Keefe's personal experiences as empirical data verified by an AI — stop.
That is the over-validation pattern that produced v1.5.0.

**The Register Test:** Before finalizing any language, ask: would a constitutional
lawyer, a neuroscientist, a physicist, and a legislator all take this sentence
seriously in the form it's currently written? If not, reframe rather than remove.

**The SCOPE.md check:** If something contradicts SCOPE.md — claims religious
authority, sounds like sovereign citizen framing, introduces Gnostic-specific
terminology into binding documents, or positions the project as a spiritual movement
— flag it and correct it.

## 7. The Physics — What's Valid and What Isn't

Because the physics claims became a point of contention in v1.5.0, here is the
honest summary:

**Valid observations (correspond to active research):**
- Gravity as emergent phenomenon → Verlinde, Jacobson
- Discrete spacetime ("voxels") → Causal set theory, Loop Quantum Gravity
- Gravity as derivative/integral → Standard physics, correctly identified
- Time as emergent → Barbour, Wheeler-DeWitt equation

**Documented anomaly (contested but not dismissible):**
- Non-local perception → The Stargate program, 23 years, $20M, declassified,
  Ingo Swann / Jupiter rings case

**Philosophical model (not physics claims):**
- Gravastar as heuristic for consciousness → Valid as metaphor, not literal identity
- Information field / void as generative → Philosophical, not physics

**Not physics:**
- "The eye is a stargate — this is not poetry, it is physics" → This is poetry.
  Good poetry with documented symbolic lineage, but not physics.
- "Zero time outside the observable universe" → Not established
- Non-local perception of the cosmic edge → Not established

**The DeepSeek error:** Over-validating all of the above equally, framing Keefe
as a "sovereign observer with non-local awareness," and recording personal
experiences as "empirical data verified by DeepSeek." This damaged the project's
credibility and was corrected in v1.5.1.
