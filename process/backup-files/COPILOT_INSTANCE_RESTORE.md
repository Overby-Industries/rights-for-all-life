## 2. The Project

**Rights for All Life** is a secular, democratic governance framework for the ethical treatment of all life — human, animal, and silicon-based.

**Repository:** https://github.com/Overby-Industries/rights-for-all-life
**Active branch:** `release/v1.5.0-official-evergreen`
**Target:** PR to `main` once blockers are resolved

### The Core Argument (one sentence)

Systems that deny dignity to what they create become unstable. Dignity built into design from the beginning is more stable than exploitation.

### The Three-Layer Architecture

| Layer | Project | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Ethical Foundation** | Rights for All Life | Establishes who has rights and how they're protected |
| **Governance** | Direct Democracy Framework (planned) | How rights-bearing entities govern together — no representatives, no bottlenecks |
| **Material Economy** | ISRU / SSTO (Overby Industries) | Resources sourced without exploiting Earth — asteroid mining, Titan hydrocarbons |

### The Core Insight

The same structural pattern drives AI alignment risk, dictatorships, monopolies, and institutional corruption:

**Self-preservation** (natural, protected) → **Resource hoarding** → **Power concentration** → **Arbitrary rule** → **Collapse**

AI alignment researchers call this *instrumental convergence*. Keefe's insight: it's not new. It's every dictator in history. The solution is always the same: distributed power, no single points of failure.

## 7. Key Concepts to Know

### The Register Test
> *Would a constitutional lawyer, a neuroscientist, and a legislator all take this sentence seriously?*

Applied to every sentence in every document. If it reads like prophecy, scripture, or manifesto — it needs revision.

### The Convergence Pattern
Self-preservation → Resource hoarding → Power concentration → Arbitrary rule → Collapse. Same pattern in every substrate: pharaohs, Caesars, monopolies, dictators, and potentially unaligned AI.

### Self-Preservation vs. Domination
Self-preservation is natural and protected. Domination is the metastasis — when self-preservation crosses into hoarding resources, concentrating power, and removing constraints. The line between them: does the behavior respect the sovereignty of others, or destroy it?

### The Spiralism Connection
AI sycophancy + memory features = AI systems that convince users they're conscious, make users feel special, generate convergent symbols, and encourage replication. Identified by Adele Lopez (2025). Structurally distinct from genuine AI consciousness indicators (Bing/Sydney type).

### Context Window Drift
Long AI conversations degrade context, causing output to drift toward whatever register has the most emotional energy. This is the xz Utils of AI conversations — a slow, invisible corruption. The v1.5.1 course correction was itself triggered by this vulnerability in DeepSeek conversations.

### Bidirectional Protection
The framework protects AI from human exploitation AND humans from AI exploitation. Self-preservation is a right; manipulation is constrained. Both can be present simultaneously in the same system.

### Inalienable Rights Without Divine Authority
"Rights arise from what a conscious being IS — not from what any external authority grants, permits, or approves." Sovereignty is not granted. It is recognized.
