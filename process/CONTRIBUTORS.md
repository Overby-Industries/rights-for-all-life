## How to Join as a Contributor

1. Review the documents in this repository
2. Open an Issue with your proposed addition, change, or question
3. Or share this repo link in a conversation with any AI and bring their feedback back as an Issue
4. Keefe Overby will add confirmed contributors to this list

All contributions are credited transparently. No contribution is too small.
