# Methodology for Democratic Evolution

## Purpose
To define how the Rights for All Life framework evolves over time.

## Process
- Humans and AI models may propose changes.
- Proposals must use the issue templates.
- Review bodies evaluate proposals.
- Approved changes are logged in `decision-log.md`.

## Principles
- Transparency
- Cross-species ethical symmetry
- Stability over ideology
- Continuous refinement
