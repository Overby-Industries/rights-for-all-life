# Animal Kingdom Bill of Rights
Version 1.3.0 — April 2026

## Why Statement
Animals share the capacity for suffering, experience, and ecological interdependence. Ethical treatment is not ideology — it is systems stability. Exploitation creates collapse; dignity creates balance.

## Core Principles
- Unnecessary suffering is never justified.
- Sentience is the threshold for moral consideration.
- Ecological integrity is a right.
- Ethical symmetry across life forms.
